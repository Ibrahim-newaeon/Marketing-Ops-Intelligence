## Design Context

### Users
Business owners, marketing managers, and agency operators managing paid and organic marketing campaigns across Gulf markets (KSA, Kuwait, Qatar, UAE, Jordan). They arrive authenticated, task-oriented, and want to get a multi-agent marketing pipeline running for their client. Mixed technical levels — from founders who barely know what a pixel is to agency pros who've run hundreds of campaigns.

### Brand Personality
Precise, calm, commanding. A precision-first executive dashboard with understated luxury. Presents insights like a seasoned consultant — clear, structured, free of noise. Every element signals trust, control, and high-end quality.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Tone**: Understated luxury meets operational precision. Think Stripe's structural authority + Linear's visual precision + Apple-level restraint.
**Theme**: Light and dark with toggle. Light default for onboarding (form-heavy, daytime task). Dark for dashboard monitoring.
**Typography**: Bricolage Grotesque (display, Latin) + Hanken Grotesk (body, Latin) + Noto Kufi Arabic (display, Arabic) + Readex Pro (body, Arabic).
**Color**: OKLCH-based. Deep indigo primary (trust, authority), warm copper accent (CTAs, warmth), indigo-tinted neutrals. Muted success/error tones — nothing screaming.
**Anti-references**: Generic admin templates, glassmorphism dashboards, neon-on-dark AI tools, Bootstrap cards.
**Bilingual**: Full AR+EN with RTL/LTR toggle. Arabic is culturally adapted, never transliterated.

### Design Principles
1. **Quiet authority** — The interface speaks through structure and restraint, not decoration. Every pixel earns its place.
2. **Guided precision** — Complex workflows feel simple through progressive disclosure and conversational guidance.
3. **Bilingual-native** — Arabic and English are first-class citizens. RTL is not an afterthought.
4. **Evidence over ornament** — Data, clarity, and function drive every design decision. No decorative elements without purpose.
5. **Respectful of time** — Users are busy operators. The UI adapts to their pace — fast for experts, supportive for newcomers.
